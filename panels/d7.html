Connecting the services

Outline ~4-5 days The microservices moment
⌥
This is an outline. We'll flesh it out together after Part 1.

The payoff of building two services: now they talk. The customer service, when it needs a document uploaded as part of onboarding, calls the document service over HTTP. This is where "microservices" stops being a word and becomes something you've actually built.

What you'll build (sketch)

  • In the customer service, an IDocumentsApiClient (defined in its Application layer) and a typed-client implementation using IHttpClientFactory.
  • An onboarding flow where the customer service receives a document, calls the document service to store it, and keeps only a reference.
  • The rule made real: the customer service never touches the document database or blob storage directly. It always goes through the document service's Api.

What's new compared to everything before

  • Service-to-service HTTP with typed clients (and why not raw new HttpClient()).
  • What happens when the other service is down, and how the caller experiences that.
  • Service contracts: how the two agree on document IDs and statuses.
